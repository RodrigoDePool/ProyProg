You will be asked a few
questions.
Each question has some
possible answers , each one
with an associated key (a,
b, c...) that will be shown
to you.

To answer to a question,
just press the associated
key.

To win the minigame, you
have to guess the correct 
answer to every question.

Press 'q' to quit game.
